# Code of Conduct

Shorty is an open source project for people who want a reliable, local-first
macOS shortcut tool. Participation should stay practical, respectful, and
focused on the work.

Expected behavior:

- Be direct without being hostile.
- Keep criticism specific to code, behavior, documentation, or process.
- Respect maintainers' time by including reproduction steps and relevant
  environment details.
- Avoid harassment, personal attacks, threats, and discriminatory language.

Maintainers may edit, close, hide, or remove contributions that make the project
less usable or less safe for participants.

Report conduct concerns to `shorty@peyton.app`.
